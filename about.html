We don't generalize.
We specialize.

Market Chemica Associates is an M&A advisory and management consulting firm built around a single, deliberate focus: the fine and specialty chemicals universe. We don't dabble in tech. We don't sell SaaS. We close deals where the chemistry, the regulatory pathway, and the operational fingerprint actually matter.

01 · Premise

Chemical fluency. Financial expertise.
This is all we do.

When a fine or specialty chemicals business goes to market, the difference between a generic M&A advisor and a specialist is the difference between summarizing a CIM and reading the EHS dossier behind it.

Why MCh

  • Founder & partner-led on every mandate
  • Operators speaking to operators
  • Deal teams assembled per asset
  • Direct relationships in NA, EU, India, China

Founded in 2012, Market Chemica Associates was built on a deliberate refusal to be a generalist boutique. Every senior person in our network has spent decades inside the businesses that hire us — running R&D at Dow, leading manufacturing at Apotex, formulating at Union Carbide, raising capital for life-sciences ventures, building catalysis platforms, scaling drug master files, structuring carve-outs of specialty assets that other bankers couldn't decipher.

That depth changes how a deal moves. We originate from operator networks, not databases. We diligence with people who have personally signed off on similar plants. We negotiate around the chemistry, the IP, and the regulatory path — not just the multiple. And when integration begins, we know what is actually difficult to merge versus what looks easy on a slide.

The result, across more than a decade and deals from $10 million to $10 billion, is a record of mandates that closed because the right specialists were in the room — not because the deck was glossy.

02 · The four pillars

What you actually get when you hire MCh.

01

1000+ years of operating experience.

Combined across our team of associates and partners. Every cluster you'd ever need to diligence — from heterocycle chemistry and chromatography to xanthan gum and vapor deposition — has someone in our network who has run a plant or led the lab.

02

30+ executive associates.

Former C-suite and senior operators across pharma, materials, agchem, F&F, cosmetic ingredients, and adjacent verticals. Engaged on a deal-specific basis. Always commercial. Never theoretical.

03

Value-added (bio)chemicals only.

Intermediates and active ingredients are our home turf. We avoid commodity petrochemicals and consumer-only formulations — and we focus where intellectual property, regulatory pathway, and process knowledge create durable value.

04

360° global reach.

Direct presence and active mandates across North America and Europe. India and Greater China through long-standing on-the-ground partners. The chemistry world isn't local — neither are we.

03 · Who we serve

Five client archetypes. One playbook.

01 / Operators

Lower-mid SMEs.

Founder- and family-owned chemicals businesses preparing for sale, succession, or strategic capital — often for the first and only time.

02 / Sponsors

PE & SFOs.

Private equity, family offices, and strategic capital groups who need a chemistry-fluent advisor for due diligence, sourcing, and value creation.

03 / HNW

Special situations.

High- and ultra-high-net-worth individuals taking direct positions in chemistry-led assets, including carve-outs, distressed turns, and minority placements.

04 / Partners

Generalist banks.

Investment banks and intermediaries who lack in-house chemistry expertise on a specific mandate and partner with us for technical diligence and operator access.
