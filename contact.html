02 · What to expect

Three things that happen next.

01 · Within 1 business day

A partner replies.

The first response comes from one of the five partners — not an analyst, not a chatbot. We confirm receipt and propose a 30-minute call.

02 · The intro call

Thirty honest minutes.

No deck. We listen, ask the chemistry-specific questions, and tell you what we'd actually do if engaged. Mutual NDA available before any specifics.

03 · The fit check

Yes, no, or referral.

If we're the right advisor, we scope an engagement. If not, we'll often refer to someone in our network who is. We say no when no is the right answer.

04 · Discretion

Full confidentiality.

Active mandates, pre-mandate conversations, and exploratory discussions are kept strictly confidential. Many of the deals we're proudest of will never appear on this site.
