Six stages.
One mandate.

A turnkey M&A process built for chemistry-led assets — from the first non-disclosure to post-merger integration. Every stage led by the partner who answered your first call. No analyst funnels, no handoffs, no surprises.

01 · The flow

Preparation through integration.

01 Preparation Align & ready Strategic intent, mandate scope, valuation hypothesis. Data-room build, financial cleanup, management positioning, and a clean technology and IP narrative — before a single buyer or seller sees the asset.
02 Targeting Originate & identify Proprietary opportunity origination through 30+ executive associates. Curated buyer or seller universe, sequenced outreach, and direct-from-operator conversations that don't appear in any database.
03 Negotiation Structure & price LOIs, term sheets, deal mechanics, earn-outs, IP carve-outs, regulatory milestones — built around the chemistry, the technology, and the operational reality. Not a generic playbook.
04 Due Diligence Validate & pressure-test Operational, regulatory, IP, EHS, environmental, commercial, and chemistry diligence — co-led by deal-specific industry experts who have personally signed off on similar plants, similar molecules, similar dossiers.
05 Closing Coordinate & execute Strategic, financial, legal, and tax advisor coordination. Drive to signing without losing the technical thread or letting deal mechanics override the underlying value rationale.
06 Post-Merger Integrate & harmonize Operational integration. R&D harmonization. Supply-chain consolidation. Regulatory file alignment. The unglamorous work after the wire transfer that determines whether the synergies on the model deck are real.
02 · Engagement models

Pick the scope that fits.

Every mandate is sized to the situation. Three engagement formats — full advisory, à la carte consulting, and referral arrangements — give clients the right level of MCh involvement without paying for unused scope.

Engagement A

Full M&A advisory.

Soup-to-nuts execution. We carry the mandate from intent to integration, coordinating every external advisor and owning the technical narrative end-to-end.

  • End-to-end six-stage process
  • Partner-led on every workstream
  • Coordinates legal, tax, financial
  • Best for first-time sellers, complex carve-outs, sponsor mandates
Engagement B

À la carte consulting.

Targeted scope. Ideal when you have a banker but need chemistry-specific muscle on a single workstream — diligence, valuation, target screen, integration plan.

  • Discrete workstream or phase
  • Plug into your existing deal team
  • Technical, regulatory, operational depth
  • Best for generalist banks, sponsors with in-house teams, intermediaries
Engagement C

M&A referral.

When the right answer is to introduce, not to lead. We connect mandates to the right banker, lawyer, or sponsor in our network — fast, on the right terms, no friction.

  • Curated introductions
  • Pre-vetted counterparties
  • Optional ongoing advisory role
  • Best for off-mandate situations or out-of-scope assets
03 · Coordination

One quarterback. Many specialists.

On a typical mandate, MCh coordinates strategic, financial, and legal advisors — and stays in the chair from intent to integration so the chemistry never falls through the cracks between specialists.

  • Strategic advisors

    Industry experts and former operators who can read a process flow diagram, debate a synthesis route, or red-team a regulatory filing.

  • Financial advisors

    Bankers, valuation specialists, and accounting partners — selected against the deal type, geography, and counterparty profile.

  • Legal advisors

    M&A counsel with chemistry-sector chops — IP transfers, FDA/EMA filings, environmental liabilities, cross-border tax structuring.

Profitable.

Capturing the technical, IP, and operational value that generalist advisors leave on the table.
