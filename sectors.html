Where the chemistry actually lives.

Eight sub-sectors. Seven deep knowledge clusters. We've been hands-on inside every one of them — running plants, scaling drug master files, formulating products that ship to consumers, and licensing the IP that holds the whole thing together.

01 · Where we sit

The fine & specialty middle of the value chain.

Upstream · Chemical Industry Commodities Simple substances derived from oil or gas.
Mch · Fine & Specialty Intermediates Commodities transformed into simple molecules.
Mch · Fine & Specialty Active Ingredients Intermediates react to complex molecules — e.g. fragrances, APIs.
Downstream · Consumer Markets B2C Products Active ingredients blended with excipients — e.g. perfumes, FDFs.

Where we work → the technical and commercial midstream where intellectual property, regulatory pathway, and process knowledge create durable value. Outside this band, the math tends to be pure capacity and price — and we leave that to the generalists.

03 · Knowledge clusters

Where we've put on a lab coat.

Cluster 01

Pharmaceutical early intermediates, RMS, API, FDF.

Drug substance to drug product — manufacturing, regulatory, and the analytical backbone that the FDA and EMA actually read.

Supply-chain management Risk assessment CDMO operations New Drug Applications Drug Master Files Catalysts & heterocycles Chromatography Spectroscopy

Cluster 02

Specialty coatings, adhesives & surface treatment.

From semiconductor-grade plasma chemistry to industrial surface engineering for the next generation of electronics and energy assets.

Plasma chemistry Metal surface treatment Vapor deposition Colloidal silica Electronics chemistry CASE applications

Cluster 03

Specialty polymers & composite materials.

Ecofriendly, biobased, biodegradable. Processes from polymerization through wood-pulp valorization. Where new materials are actually winning.

Biobased polymers Biodegradable materials Polymerization Catalysis Polymer processing Wood-pulp materials Composite formulations

Cluster 04

Specialty surface cleaning & disinfecting.

Surgical instruments, skin, hair, hard surfaces. The chemistry behind biocides, surfactants, and the formulations that actually work in regulated environments.

Chelation Renewable solvents Surfactants & ethoxylates Emulsification Defoaming Biocides Hydrogen peroxide Glutaraldehyde

Cluster 05

Rheology modification.

Personal care, beverages, liquid nutrition. The hydrocolloid and gelation chemistry that controls texture, mouthfeel, and shelf-life across consumer brands.

Hydrocolloids Xanthan gum Acacia gum Gelation chemistry Personal care Liquid nutrition

Cluster 06

Chloro-alkali & derivatives.

Medical materials, packaging, and the disinfecting backbone behind hospitals and water systems. Big-tonnage chemistry with high regulatory stakes.

PVC Chloromethanes Bleach systems Medical materials Packaging Industrial disinfection

Cluster 07

Dietary supplements & nutrition.

Wellness beverages, liquid supplements, the nutraceutical universe. Adaptogens, micronutrients, and the natural-preservation chemistry that holds clean-label products together.

Wellness beverages Liquid supplements Nutraceuticals Natural preservation Adaptogens Micronutrients Carboxymethylcellulose
